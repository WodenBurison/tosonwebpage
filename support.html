Support the Work

Keep the Peace

The Other Side of Normal is a free supplement. If it's been useful at your table — if your Folk community has a neighborhood you keep coming back to, or a character whose distinction you had to workshop three times to get right — consider buying a coffee. It goes directly toward more weird little games like this one.

Support on Ko-fi

Opens in a new tab · No account required · Pay what you want

What Ko-fi supports

Development of future TOSON material, including the planned Folk Files supplements — one per Folk Type, with expanded mechanics, lore, and SFX options.

It also supports the time it takes to maintain this SRD as the game evolves.

The book on DriveThruRPG

The Other Side of Normal is available pay-what-you-want on DriveThruRPG. If you want to support the work without Ko-fi, grabbing the PDF and leaving a rating is another great way to help.

While you're there: if you don't own the Cortex Prime Game Handbook, it's where the base rules live. TOSON assumes you have it.

Play it and tell me how it went

Honestly? That's the best support. If something clicked at your table — or broke in an interesting way — I want to hear about it.

You can reach me through Ko-fi or leave a comment on DriveThruRPG.
